How to Play

Welcome to Battle of Emoji, a turn-based strategy game where you command powerful emojis!

Mechanics

  • Turn-Based Combat: You and the AI take turns using moves. Faster moves (lower cooldown) can be used more often.
  • Stamina: Every move costs Stamina. Stamina regenerates slightly at the end of every round. Manage it wisely!
  • Cooldowns: Powerful moves need time to recharge.
  • Critical Hits: There is a 10% chance for any attack to deal CRITICAL (1.7x) damage.
  • Dodge Chance: Every character has a base 25% chance to dodge attacks. Some moves and buffs increase this.

Weather Effects

The weather on the battlefield can turn the tide of battle:

  • Sunny: Fire moves deal 10% more damage.
  • Rain: Fire moves deal 20% less damage.
  • Storm: Electric moves deal 40% more damage!
  • Fog: All attacks have a 15% higher chance to miss.

Special Abilities

Each character has a unique special ability that can be a game-changer. (Currently integrated into move sets).
